What are Ragdoll Cats?

Ragdoll cats are a breed known for their striking blue eyes, semi-long silky coat, and large, muscular build.

Ragdolls come in a variety of colors and patterns, including seal, blue, chocolate, and lilac, with patterns like colorpoint, mitted, and bicolor.

They typically have a gentle and calm demeanor, often described as "dog-like" because of their tendency to follow their owners around and their ease in being handled and cuddled.
